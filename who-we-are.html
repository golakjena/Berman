Jill Asrael

Principal, CFO, CCO
Download vCard

Jill Asrael joined Berman Capital Advisors in 2011 as a Principal. She serves as the firm’s Chief Financial Officer and Chief Compliance Officer and as a client relationship manager. Since 2001, she has been the CFO of Grapevine Partners, a single family office and now a BCA client, where she has managed all of the family’s financial interests including investment managers, tax professionals, insurance professionals and estate planning attorneys. In addition, she managed an operating company and a charitable supporting organization for the family. Jill also serves as the CFO for Peachtree II, L.P., an SBIC mezzanine debt fund in Atlanta.

Jill’s prior experience includes two years in the Corporate Turnaround group at Arthur Andersen performing financial analysis and cash flow forecasting and working with management to implement new policies and procedures to improve operations. Jill also worked for five years at Larson-Juhl, Inc. where she was responsible for budgeting and forecasting as well as financial statement preparation and treasury functions.

Jill graduated with a B.A. in Political Science from Washington University in St. Louis and has been a licensed CPA since 1998.

JUSTIN BERMAN

Founder & Chief Executive Officer
Download vCard

Justin Berman founded Berman Capital Advisors in 2010 as part of his commitment to provide truly objective family office services, wealth management, and investment consulting services to a select group of high-net-worth families.

Prior to launching BCA, Justin was a Vice-President in the Private Wealth Management division of Goldman Sachs & Co., where he was a member of the firm’s Leadership Council. He has also served in the Investment Advisory Group at myCFO, Inc. and in the Private Client Group at Arthur Andersen & Co.

Justin is a Summa Cum Laude graduate of Georgetown University where he served as Varsity Tennis Captain and was a Rhodes Scholar Finalist. He also holds an MBA from The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania where he was named a Palmer Scholar.

Justin is active in community affairs and is on the board of trustees of The McDonough School of Business at Georgetown University, Leadership Atlanta, Junior Achievement of Georgia, and is an ambassador for Grady Hospital. Justin is a board member of Nolan Transportation Group and Victory Park Capital. Justin is also involved in the Young Presidents’ Organization. He and his wife, Mara, reside in Atlanta and have three children.
